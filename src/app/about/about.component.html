What is AnimeDoro?

AnimeDoro is a time management technique similar to the pomodoro technique. In the pomodoro method, the work cycle is broken up into 25 minute chunks of work separated by 5 minute breaks. Each of these cycles is considered 1 pomodoro. After 4 pomodoros, a break of 20-25 minute is taken. With AnimeDoro, instead of short work periods and short breaks, the work cycle is broken up into 1 hr work chunks with a longer 20 minute break. With the longer break time, users of this technique can do other things to relax themselves, such as watching an episode of anime. Why AnimeDoro Use AnimeDoro if you love anime, but have to still get things done. With AnimeDoro, you can get all your work done while also watching all the anime you enjoy. This website will help you time your work and break sessions and keep track of all the AnimeDoro cycles.
